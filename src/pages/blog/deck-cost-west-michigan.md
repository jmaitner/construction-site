If you're planning a deck in Grand Rapids or anywhere in West Michigan, the first question is almost always the same: **what's this going to cost?** Below is an honest, local breakdown for 2026 — no national averages that don't reflect Michigan labor and material prices.

## What is the average cost of a deck in West Michigan?

Most decks we build in the Grand Rapids area land between **$45 and $75 per square foot installed**, depending on material and complexity. As a rough guide:

| Deck size | Pressure-treated | Composite / PVC |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Small (under 200 sq ft) | $6,000 – $10,000 | $9,000 – $15,000 |
| Medium (200–400 sq ft) | $10,000 – $18,000 | $16,000 – $28,000 |
| Large (400–600 sq ft) | $18,000 – $30,000 | $28,000 – $45,000+ |

These are ballpark ranges for finished, code-compliant decks — not just the lumber.

## What affects the price most?

- **Material.** Pressure-treated pine is the most affordable; cedar, composite (like Trex), and PVC cost more but need less upkeep.
- **Height and stairs.** A ground-level deck is far cheaper than a second-story deck with multiple stair runs and taller footings.
- **Railings.** Cable and aluminum railings cost more than wood.
- **Site conditions.** Sloped yards, poor access, and tearing out an old deck all add labor.
- **Add-ons.** Lighting, built-in seating, pergolas, and under-deck drainage raise the total.

## Why are Michigan decks priced the way they are?

Our freeze-thaw winters are hard on outdoor structures. Footings have to go below the frost line, fasteners need to resist corrosion, and cheaper materials simply don't last. Paying a bit more up front for proper footings and quality decking usually costs **less over the life of the deck** than rebuilding a cut-rate one in five years.

## Does a deck add value to your home?

Yes. A well-built deck is one of the better outdoor returns on investment, and in lake-country West Michigan, usable outdoor space is a real selling point. The key word is *well-built* — a sagging, graying deck does the opposite.
